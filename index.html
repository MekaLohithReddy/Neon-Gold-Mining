Neon Gold Mining

Find 12 Safe Tiles!

Game Rules

Welcome to Mines! The objective of the game is to open safe tiles without hitting any bombs.

  • There is a 5x5 grid of tiles.
  • There are 3 hidden bombs randomly placed on the grid.
  • Your goal is to open at least 12 safe tiles to win the game.
  • If you click on a bomb, the game ends, and you lose!
  • If you win or lose, a "Play Again" button will appear.
